Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Oreniq Games handles information for Cavern Veerfall and other titles that link to it.

Effective date: April 26, 2026

This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when it may be shared, and how to contact us with privacy questions.

Oreniq Games ("we," "us," or "our") operates the mobile game Cavern Veerfall, currently distributed on Android under the package com.oreniq.endlessdodge. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, retain, and protect information when you use the game, related support channels, and connected services.

1. Information We Collect

Gameplay and local device data

We store gameplay and settings data on your device so the game can function. This may include best score, coins, consumable inventory, missions, progression, daily systems, tutorial state, and settings preferences.

Service and analytics data

When online services are enabled, we and our service providers may collect technical and gameplay information such as service-generated identifiers, session identifiers, timestamps, run starts, run finishes, reward claims, review or share taps, purchase attempts, purchase results, app version, device model, operating system, language, and region.

Authentication data

Cavern Veerfall uses Unity Authentication and may sign players in anonymously to support connected Unity services.

Purchase and transaction data

If you make in-app purchases, we may receive transaction-related information such as product identifiers, purchase state, and entitlement state. Payment card details are handled by Google Play and its payment providers, not by us.

Support communications

If you contact us, we may collect your email address, the contents of your message, attachments you choose to send, and related troubleshooting information.

Optional diagnostic and pre-release testing data

Limited test builds may include additional diagnostics such as tester names, feedback notes, gameplay recordings, device details, run metrics, build metadata, or uploaded test bundles when a tester knowingly participates in a QA workflow.

2. How We Use Information

  • Operate the game and save progress.
  • Authenticate players and support connected services.
  • Analyze gameplay balance, retention, feature clarity, and monetization flows.
  • Process purchases and grant virtual content.
  • Respond to support requests, refund questions, and privacy inquiries.
  • Debug bugs, crashes, performance issues, and device compatibility problems.
  • Review voluntary tester feedback and diagnostics from internal or pre-release QA.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3. How We Share Information

We may share information with service providers that help us operate the game, including Unity Gaming Services and Google Play services; platform providers and payment processors; professional advisers; and authorities or counterparties when required by law or a business transaction.

We do not sell personal information for money.

4. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including to operate the game, maintain player entitlements, investigate abuse, comply with legal obligations, and review voluntary QA submissions. Local gameplay data usually remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app storage.

5. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or restriction of certain personal information. You may also uninstall the app, limit permissions through device settings, and contact us to request deletion of identifiable support or QA materials when applicable.
