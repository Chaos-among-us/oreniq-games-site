## Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** `[effective-date]`

`[legal-entity-name]` ("we," "us," or "our") operates the mobile game `Cavern Veerfall` (currently packaged as `com.oreniq.endlessdodge`) on Google Play.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and retain information when you use the game, our related support channels, and any linked services we operate for the game.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Gameplay and local device data
We store gameplay and settings data on your device so the game can function properly. This may include:
- best score
- coins and consumable inventory
- mission, challenge, reward, mastery, and progression state
- tutorial and settings preferences
- haptics and similar local options

This local save data is generally stored on your device through Unity and platform storage mechanisms such as `PlayerPrefs`.

### B. Service and analytics data
When online services are enabled, we and our service providers may collect technical and gameplay information needed to operate, secure, analyze, and improve the game. This may include:
- service-generated player or installation identifiers
- session identifiers
- event timestamps
- gameplay events such as run starts, run finishes, rewards claimed, review/share taps, and purchase attempts/results
- app version, device model, operating system, language, region, and similar technical data made available by the platform or service provider

The current project uses Unity services including Unity Analytics and Unity Authentication.

### C. Authentication data
The current project uses Unity Authentication and may sign players in anonymously to support Unity services. This may create or use a service-managed player identifier even if you do not create a traditional username/password account.

### D. Purchase and transaction data
If you make in-app purchases, we may receive limited transaction-related information such as:
- product identifiers
- whether a purchase succeeded, failed, or was deferred
- entitlement state for purchased content

Payment card details and most payment processing information are handled by Google Play and its payment providers, not by us.

### E. Support communications
If you contact us for support, bug reporting, billing help, or legal/privacy questions, we may collect:
- your email address
- the content of your message
- attachments or screenshots you choose to send
- related troubleshooting information you provide

### F. Internal QA and test-build data
Internal QA builds may collect more information than the public release build. If you use an internal QA build, we may collect:
- tester name
- post-run survey responses
- freeform tester notes
- gameplay screen recordings
- device model and operating system
- run metrics and build metadata
- uploaded QA ZIP bundles and reports

If these QA-only features are removed before public launch, this section can be narrowed in the public-facing version of the policy.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:
- operate the game and save progress
- authenticate and support connected services
- analyze gameplay balance, retention, feature clarity, and monetization flows
- process purchases and grant virtual content
- respond to support requests and refund questions
- debug crashes, bugs, and gameplay issues
- improve the design, stability, performance, and economy of the game
- review internal QA submissions and tester recordings
- comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

## 3. How We Share Information

We may share information with:
- service providers that help us operate the game, such as Unity services and Google Play billing infrastructure
- platform providers when you install, purchase, or use the game through their stores or operating systems
- professional advisers or authorities when required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, or the integrity of the service
- a buyer, investor, or successor in connection with a merger, financing, acquisition, or sale of assets

We do not sell personal information for money.

## 4. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including to:
- operate the game and maintain player entitlements
- investigate fraud, security issues, or abuse
- comply with tax, accounting, refund, and legal obligations
- evaluate internal QA results and fix bugs

Local gameplay data usually remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app storage. Internal QA artifacts may be retained until the test cycle is complete and analysis is finished, unless a longer retention period is required by law or operational need.

## 5. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or restriction of certain personal information.

You may also:
- stop using the game at any time
- uninstall the app
- limit permissions through your device settings
- contact us to request deletion of support records or internal QA submissions that identify you

Requests can be sent to:
- Support: `oreniqgames@gmail.com`
- Privacy: `oreniqgames@gmail.com`

If the released game later adds a user-creatable account system, we will review whether additional account-deletion flows are required in-app and on the web.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The current launch assumption is that the game is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.

If you later decide to target children or families, update this section and re-check Google Play Families and target-audience requirements before release.

## 7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

The public release build should use production-grade transport security for any network submission involving user data.

## 8. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than where you live. Those countries may have different data-protection laws than your jurisdiction.

## 9. Changes To This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and take other steps as required by applicable law.
